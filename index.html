What I Do

Business Understanding

I collaborate with stakeholders to understand the data requirements and business objectives, as well as define the problem statement.

Data Understanding

I explore ERM data to identify relevant variables, data types and relationships, as well as conduct data quality checks to identify errors, inconsistencies, and missing values.

Data Preparation

Data Modelling

Develop data extraction algorithms and rules to ensure accuracy and completeness. Validate extracted data against gold standard datasets.

Data Deployment
